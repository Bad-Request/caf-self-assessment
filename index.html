      Outcome grid — every dot is one of the 41 contributing outcomes; fill colour shows its current status, border colour (if set) shows its baseline target; select a dot to jump to it

      How the suggested status works: open an outcome and tick the Indicators of Good Practice that genuinely describe your organisation. Tick even one "Not achieved" indicator and the outcome is suggested as Not achieved, whatever else is ticked — an outcome can't score higher than its lowest ticked indicator. Otherwise, ticking every "Achieved" indicator suggests Achieved; ticking some (but not all) achieved indicators, or any "Partially achieved" indicator, suggests Partially achieved. You can override the suggestion at any time using the buttons beside each outcome — the CAF is explicit that IGPs support expert judgement rather than replace it.
